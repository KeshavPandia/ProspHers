Things To Remember

1. Don't fall for the word free.
2. Check that majority of people are buying the product.
3. Don't buy the product if someone is pressurising you to buy it.
4. High return investments are subject to market risk.
5. Consistent returns should be checked as the markets keep on fluctuating.
6. Read all the documents carefully before moving forward at any stage.
7. Make sure that the investment scheme is regulated SEBI.
8. Foreign investments have a huge probability of losses.
