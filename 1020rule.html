50-20-30 Rule

The new secret to save !

A budget allows you to save money for your financial goals. To begin, you should use the 50-30-20 budgeting rule. Set aside 50% of your monthly income for necessities, 30% for discretionary spending, and save the remaining 20% for long-term objectives. Set aside enough money in six to eight months for an emergency fund.

Track your expenses

Effective financial planning entails being aware of where your money is going. Make a list of where and how much you spend each month. You will quickly identify costs that you can avoid. Nowadays, budgeting apps make it even easier to track expenses.

Use Credit Cards Judiciously

Cancel any subscriptions or memberships that are no longer in use, especially if they renew automatically. Credit cards might tempt you to spend above your means, accruing interest on past-due balances. Use it only in emergencies and opt for digital money wherever feasible.
